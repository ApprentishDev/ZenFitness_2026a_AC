Head Office

    Located in the scenic landscape of Northland, our Whangarei Head Office serves as the central hub for Zen Fitness operations across New Zealand. More than just an administrative center, this facility embodies our commitment to holistic health, blending modern architectural design with a peaceful, community-focused atmosphere.

    From this location, we coordinate our specialized fitness programs, manage our nationwide branches, and continue our mission to bring "Zen" into the active lives of New Zealanders. Whether you're visiting for an inquiry or a training session, you’ll find a team dedicated to excellence, integrity, and your personal fitness journey.





BRANCHES

North Shore Branch

    Experience fitness with a coastal breeze. Our North Shore facility focuses on high-intensity training andcommunity wellness, offering a spacious layout designed to help you reach your peak performance in the heart of the Shore.

Hamilton Branch

    Fuel your ambition at our Hamilton hub. Strategically located for the city’s go-getters, this branch features state-of-the-art strength equipment and a high-energy atmosphere perfect for those looking to crush their daily fitness goals.

Panmure Branch

    Our Panmure NZ favorite! Located in the heart of East Auckland, this branch offers a welcoming, "whānau-first" environment. With a mix of traditional lifting and modern functional zones, it’s the perfect place for locals to train, sweat, and succeed.
