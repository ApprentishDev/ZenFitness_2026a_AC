The Company

      Zen Fitness was founded on the belief that physical strength and mental clarity are two sides of the same coin. In a world that is often chaotic and fast-paced, we provide a sanctuary where members can reconnect with their bodies through intentional movement and mindful practice. We don't just focus on "dropping ten"—we focus on helping you find your "Zen."

      Our philosophy is rooted in functional wellness. Whether you are flowing through a restorative Yoga session, building core stability in Pilates, or pushing your cardiovascular limits in HIIT, every movement is designed to be sustainable, empowering, and results-driven. We move away from the "no pain, no gain" mentality, opting instead for Smarter Training that respects the body’s limits while consistently challenging its potential.

      At the heart of Zen Fitness is our Community. We pride ourselves on being more than just a gym; we are a collective of expert coaches and dedicated members who support one another. From our personalized nutrition guidance to our team of specialized instructors, we are committed to helping you reclaim your health, improve your sleep, and build a body that is ready for life’s adventures.

TEAM LEADERS

Maia Henderson

   Maia specializes in contemporary Pilates with a focus on core stability and postural alignment. Her classes emphasize the mind-body connection, ensuring every movement is intentional and controlled. She is passionate about helping clients build long, lean muscle while improving overall functional strength.

Zoe Marshall

   Zoe combines traditional Pilates techniques with modern athletic conditioning to challenge both flexibility and endurance. She focuses heavily on breathwork and spinal mobility to help members recover from daily desk-bound fatigue. Her sessions are designed to empower you with a stronger, more resilient foundation for everyday life.

Hamish Douglas

    Hamish brings an explosive yet sustainable energy to high-intensity interval training, prioritizing form over speed. He uses a science-based approach to metabolic conditioning to help you push your cardiovascular limits without risking burnout. Expect a session that is as much about mental grit as it is about physical power.

Alistair Boyd

    Alistair leads restorative Vinyasa flows focused on anatomical alignment and deep stretching to release tension. His practice emphasizes "finding your Zen" through steady, mindful movement. It is a sanctuary for those seeking to balance physical strength with mental clarity and inner peace.
