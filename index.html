Pilates

   Strengthen your body and calm your mind with our Pilates classes at Zen Fitness. Designed for all fitness levels, Pilates focuses on controlled movements, core strength, and mindful breathing to improve overall body balance and flexibility.

   Our sessions help enhance posture, build lean muscle, and increase mobility while remaining low-impact—making it ideal for beginners, athletes, and those recovering from injuries. Regular practice can also reduce stress, improve coordination, and support long-term physical well-being.

HIIT

High Intensity Interval Training

   Boost your strength, endurance, and energy with our HIIT classes. Focused on short bursts of intense exercise and active recovery, these sessions are designed to maximise results in a short time. Suitable for all fitness levels, you’ll feel stronger, fitter, and more energised after every class.

Yoga

   Improve your flexibility, balance, and overall well-being with our Yoga classes. Focused on mindful movement and controlled breathing, these sessions help reduce stress while strengthening both body and mind. Suitable for all fitness levels, you’ll feel more relaxed, centred, and refreshed after every class.
